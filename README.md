# ❤️ Heart Disease Prediction (Data Wrangling & Machine Learning)

### 📌 Overview
This project analyzes a medical dataset containing 1,888 patient records to identify key physiological indicators of heart disease. It focuses on the transition from raw data cleaning to predictive modeling.

### 🎯 Objective
* Perform comprehensive **Data Wrangling** on medical datasets
* Conduct **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to uncover health patterns
* Apply **Machine Learning** to predict the presence of heart disease
* Identify significant clinical risk factors for early diagnosis

### 🛠 Tools & Technologies
* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn)
* **Jupyter Notebook** (`.ipynb`)
* **Random Forest Classifier** (Predictive Modeling)

### 📊 Key Analysis Performed
* **Data Cleaning:** Handling missing values and ensuring clinical consistency across 14 attributes
* **Outlier Detection:** Evaluating extreme readings in blood pressure and cholesterol
* **Feature Engineering:** Creating age categories and encoding categorical medical data
* **Correlation Mapping:** Identifying relationships between age, heart rate, and disease presence

### 🔍 Key Insights
* 📍 **Thalassemia & Heart Rate:** Identified as the most critical predictors of cardiac risk
* 📍 **Age Trends:** Patients aged 50–60 show the highest incidence of heart disease in this dataset
* 📍 **Gender Comparison:** Male patients exhibited higher frequency of disease, while females showed higher median heart rates
* 📍 **Clinical Accuracy:** The Random Forest model achieved a **80% accuracy** rate in predicting patient outcomes
